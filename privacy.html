Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Overview

ThreatRift ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at threatrift.com.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Contact information — If you email us voluntarily (e.g., via contact@threatrift.com), we receive the information you choose to provide.
  • Server and analytics logs — Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, referring URL, and access timestamps.
  • Analytics — We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first, cookieless analytics service that measures aggregate site traffic. It does not use cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, and does not track individuals across sites.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to respond to inquiries, operate and improve our website, and maintain security. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical log data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not control GitHub's data practices.

Data Retention

We retain contact correspondence only as long as needed to address your inquiry or as required by law. Server logs are retained according to our hosting provider's standard practices.
