ABOUT

Redefining threat operations.

ThreatRift brings autonomous threat operations to the organizations that need them most. Curated threat intelligence, proactive hunting, and detection engineering have always been the work of the largest, best-staffed security teams. We deliver that same capability as software — so organizations can run a full threat program without hiring three specialists or standing up three platforms.

We believe the answer to an overwhelmed security team isn't another tool or another console. It's running the essential work — intelligence, hunting, and detection — continuously and at machine speed, with a human always in control.
