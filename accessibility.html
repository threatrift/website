Accessibility

Effective July 2026

Our Commitment

ThreatRift is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. Everyone should be able to access information about what we do.

Conformance Target

We aim to conform with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure with landmark regions and a skip-to-content link
  • Full keyboard navigability with visible focus indicators
  • Sufficient color contrast for text and interface elements
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images and graphics
  • Support for reduced-motion preferences

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We review the site regularly and work to address issues as we find them.

Feedback & Contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, or need information in an alternative format, please email us at contact@threatrift.com. We welcome your feedback and will do our best to respond and resolve issues promptly.
